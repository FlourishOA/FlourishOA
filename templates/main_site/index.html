Welcome to the Future of Publishing

The OpenAccess movementt has changed a three hundred year old publishing model that exploits academics and allows publishers to have some of the highest profit margins of any corporations in the world.

Still, some publishers and journals take advantage of the lack of clear information about article publishing costs. With simple, no-nonsense data analysis and visualization, PriceSleuth fixes this problem.

Our Data: Philosphy and Approach

  • Current

    Many of the prices in our database are pulled directly from the publisher's webistes, updated within the last few months.

  • Archived

    Our data has journal prices for some journals as far back as 2012, and more historical prices are being added for more effective analysis.

  • Easily Updated

    We provide a simple API for authorized publishers and other known authorities to add data to our database with a simple GUI.
